What Happens After You Submit

For your reference
You don't need to do anything further — the workflow proceeds automatically. Each team receives an email with a direct link to their form at the right time. You will receive a notification email when onboarding is complete.
1
ID Setup
The ID Setup team receives an email and assigns the employee's internal ID, SiteDocs worker access, and DSS training account.
2
Safety — DSS+ & SiteDocs Learning Paths
Safety receives an email to configure the employee's DSS+ and SiteDocs learning paths and assign their site location. This happens for all employees immediately after ID Setup.
3
HR Verification
HR receives an email to verify employee details against ADP and assign the ADP Associate ID. HR may correct the name, hire date, title, or site — you and your manager will receive a notification if any changes are made. For salary employees or employees needing system/email access, this is completed before any additional setup begins. For hourly employees with no system access, HR Verification is typically completed on or near the start date.
4a
IT Confirmation — Dave Langohr
Dave Langohr receives a confirmation request whenever system or equipment access is included in the workflow. He reviews the configuration before IT Setup proceeds.
⬦ Applies when system or equipment access is requested (after HR Verification)
4b
Skipped for Hourly / No System Access
If you selected Hourly + No System Access, the workflow ends after HR Verification. No IT Setup or specialist steps are required.
⬦ Only applies to Hourly + No System Access employees
5
IT Setup
IT provisions the email account, computer, phone, and all requested system access. You and your manager receive an email with the employee's credentials and account details.
6
Specialist Action Items (parallel)
Each specialist team is notified simultaneously: Business Cards, Credit Card, Fleetio, 30/60/90 Review, Jonas/Purchasing, and WIS Assignment (sent to you as the reporting manager). These run in parallel and each team works independently.
⬦ Only the specialists matching your equipment and system selections are notified
✓
Onboarding Complete
When all specialist items are closed, you and HR receive a completion email summarizing everything that was set up. The employee is ready to start.

Common Questions

I submitted with the wrong hire date — what do I do?
HR can correct the hire date during HR Verification. Contact HR directly and let them know. They will update it and the correction is automatically logged.
I forgot to add a system — can I go back and edit?
Once submitted, the initial request cannot be edited directly. Contact IT or the relevant specialist team to add access manually, or submit an Equipment / System Request through the portal for an existing employee.
The hire date is less than 3 business days away — what do I do?
Submit the form immediately and contact HR directly to expedite. The form will still be processed but HR should be made aware of the urgent timeline so they can prioritize.
Which domain should I select for the Google Account email?
Select the same domain as the reporting manager wherever possible (e.g. if the manager is @team-group.com, the employee should also be @team-group.com). If unsure, check with HR or IT.
What is WIS Assignment?
WIS (Work Instructions & Safety) is a BOSS module. After IT Setup, you as the reporting manager will receive a WIS action item email asking you to assign the appropriate WIS modules to the employee in BOSS. This is always created regardless of what systems were selected.
I haven't received any confirmation or update emails — what do I do?
Check your spam folder first. If not there, log into the TEAM Group Employee Portal and check the workflow status yourself from the Dashboard using your Request ID. You can see the current stage and which step is pending.
