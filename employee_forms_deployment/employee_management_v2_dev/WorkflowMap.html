1. Initial Request

Hiring Manager submits request with employee details, role, site, and required access.

-

2a. ID Generation

ID Setup team creates Employee ID, DSS username, and SiteDocs record.

-

2b. HR Verification

HR confirms title, employment type, manager, and compensation.

-

3. IT Setup

IT creates Google account, assigns devices, configures system access.

-

Jonas

Accounting sets up job cost numbers.

-

Credit Card

Finance processes card application and sets limits.

-

Fleetio

Fleet team sets up vehicle access and assignment.

-

Business Cards

Admin orders physical business cards.

-

SiteDocs

Safety team adds worker to SiteDocs safety platform.

-

30/60/90

Manager and HR schedule performance review touchpoints.

-

* Steps 2a and 2b run in parallel. Specialist steps (4+) are issued as Action Items once IT Setup is submitted.

1. EOE Request

Manager submits request with term date, reason, systems access, equipment, and supporting documentation.

-

2. HR Approval

HR reviews the request and approves or rejects. Approval triggers all offboarding action items simultaneously.

-

Asset Collection

Manager collects company equipment (laptop, phone, vehicle, credit card, etc.) and records status.

-

IT Deactivation

IT deactivates Google account, BOSS, Delivery, Incidents, CAA. Configures email forwarding, vacation responder, and schedules account deletion.

-

HR Systems

HR removes ADP supervisor access and payroll records.

-

Fleet Deactivation

Fleet team removes Fleetio access and reassigns vehicles.

-

Finance / Jonas

Finance removes Jonas Purchasing and Central Purchasing access.

-

Employee Deactivation

ID Setup team deactivates SiteDocs, DSS user, and BOSS WIS module records.

-

Safety (FYI)

Safety receives an advance notification only. No action required — deactivation handled by ID Setup above.

* All offboarding action items are issued simultaneously upon HR approval. Each team works independently. Workflow completes when all items are closed.
