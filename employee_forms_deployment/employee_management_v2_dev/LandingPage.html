Employee Management Portal

Access workflows, dashboards, and administrative tools

The forms are fully guided and validated — you don’t need to read anything before submitting. Required fields are clearly marked, conditional sections appear automatically, and each form tells you exactly what to fill in. The reference guides below are here when you want context on how a workflow fits together, or to answer a specific “what happens next?” question.

Which form do I use?
Hiring someone new → New Employee Request.
Existing employee needs access or equipment → Equipment and Systems Request.
Someone is leaving → End of Employment Request.
Role, site, or manager change → Position / Site Change.
Do I need to follow up after submitting?
No. The system automatically emails each person when it’s their turn. You don’t need to contact HR or IT separately. If something stalls, the Workflow Dashboard shows exactly which step is waiting.
What if I need to change something after submitting?
Submitted requests can only be modified by an admin. Contact HR or IT directly and include the employee name and which form you submitted. Don’t re-submit — that creates a duplicate workflow.
How do I know if something is stuck?
Open the Workflow Dashboard and find the employee’s row — it shows exactly which step is waiting and who it’s assigned to. You can use the Remind button on the dashboard to re-send the notification to whoever has the open action item.
My request isn’t showing up on the dashboard.
Search by the employee’s name or email on the dashboard. If it’s missing, the submission may not have completed — check for a confirmation message when you submitted. If unsure, contact an admin before re-submitting.
