Before You Begin

What this form does: The Employee Change Status Request handles any change to an existing employee's role, site, manager, classification, or system access. You can select multiple change types at once — for example, a simultaneous site transfer and promotion. HR reviews and approves the request before any changes take effect.
When to use this form vs. others:
  • Employee leaving the company → use the End of Employment Request instead
  • Brand new employee joining → use the New Employee Request instead
  • Existing employee needs additional system or equipment access only → use the Equipment and Systems Request instead (faster, no HR approval gate)
  • Any role, site, manager, or classification change → this is the right form
Multiple changes at once: This form supports checking multiple change types simultaneously — e.g. Site Transfer + Position Change + Manager Change all in one submission. You do not need to submit separate requests.

What Happens Next

1
HR Review & Approval
HR receives the request and reviews all details — employee information, change types, new access, and any equipment. HR confirms the job title and JR title (if applicable) and either approves or rejects the request. No changes happen until HR approves.
⬦ HR may reject and request corrections or additional information
2
IT Confirmation — Dave Langohr
If the request includes any new system or equipment access, Dave Langohr reviews the BOSS and IT configuration before IT Setup proceeds.
⬦ Conditional — only if new system or equipment access was requested
3
IT Setup & Access Changes
IT provisions new system access (BOSS, Google Account, ADP, etc.), removes old access, and handles any equipment changes. Access removal and provisioning happen in the same step.
⬦ Conditional — based on what was requested and what was checked for removal
4
Specialist Action Items
Specialist teams complete their portions in parallel — credit cards, business cards, Fleetio, Jonas, 30-60-90 review setup.
⬦ Conditional — only for items that were requested
✓
Change Request Complete
All steps are marked complete. You receive a final confirmation email.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I submit multiple change types at once — e.g. a site transfer and a promotion at the same time?
Yes — this is the intended use. Check all applicable change types. The sub-sections for each will appear, and HR will review everything in a single request. You do not need to submit multiple forms.
The employee only needs new system access — no title or site change. Should I use this form?
No. For access-only additions to an existing employee, use the Equipment and Systems Request form. It has a faster workflow with no HR approval gate. This form is intended for role, site, manager, or classification changes (which may also include access updates as a side effect).
What's the difference between Section 5 (new access) and Section 9 (access removal)?
Section 5 is for systems the employee is gaining access to — new things being added. Section 9 is for systems they are losing access to — old things being removed. Both can apply in the same request. Example: an employee moving from a BOSS-enabled role to a new role that needs Jonas but not BOSS would have Jonas in Section 5 and BOSS in Section 9.
Is a JR title required for every salary employee change?
Only if the change involves a title or classification update that affects their BOSS Job Rating. If the employee is already salary with an existing JR, and their title isn't changing, you can select No. When in doubt, select Yes and choose the closest title — HR will confirm during review.
What if HR rejects the request?
You will receive an email with HR's notes explaining the rejection and what corrections are needed. The request will be re-opened for editing. Make the corrections and resubmit. No actions are taken until HR approves.
How do I check the status of my request?
Log into the TEAM Group Employee Portal and open the Dashboard. Find your request by Workflow ID (from the confirmation email) or by employee name. The dashboard shows the current step and which actions are pending.
