Before You Begin

What this form does: Submitting the End of Employment Request initiates the offboarding workflow. HR reviews the request, then IT removes system access, fleet and finance teams are notified as needed, and a checklist is generated for equipment collection.
Terminations require HR pre-approval before you submit. If the reason is "Terminated," you must confirm that HR has already approved the termination. Do not submit without that approval — the form will ask you to confirm it, and the request will be held if approval is missing.
When to use this form vs. others:
  • Employee leaving the company → this form (End of Employment Request)
  • Employee staying, role or site changing → use the Employee Change Status Request instead
  • Adding system or equipment access for a current employee → use the Equipment and Systems Request instead

Frequently Asked Questions

The employee was terminated — do I need HR approval before submitting?
Yes. When you select "Terminated" as the reason, the form asks you to confirm HR approval. Only select Yes if HR has already verbally or in writing approved the termination. Submitting without this will delay the workflow and may require a correction.
The employee resigned — do I need HR approval before submitting?
No pre-approval required. For Resigned, Retired, End of contract, and Deceased, HR approval is not required at the time of submission. HR will still review the request, but the workflow proceeds without a pre-approval gate.
I'm not sure which systems the employee had access to. What should I check?
Check anything you think they may have had. If a system is checked but the employee didn't have access, the removal team will simply note "no access found" and move on — it causes no harm. It is much better to over-select than to leave active access unremoved. If you're unsure, check BOSS, Google Account, and ADP Supervisor Access as a minimum for salary employees, and only Google Account and SiteDocs for most hourly employees.
The employee doesn't have a company email — do I leave the work email blank?
Leave the "Has Work Email" checkbox unchecked. The work email field and the Google offboarding section will not appear. Most hourly employees with no system access fall into this category.
What if I submitted the form but made a mistake?
Contact HR immediately with your Workflow ID (from the confirmation email). If HR has not yet approved, the request can be corrected. If it has already been approved and actions have started, HR will need to manually adjust affected records.
How do I check the status of the request?
Log into the TEAM Group Employee Portal and open the Dashboard. Find your request by the Workflow ID from your confirmation email. The dashboard shows which steps have been completed and which are still pending.
